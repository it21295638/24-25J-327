E-Learning App for Dyslexic Students (Ages 5–6)

Project ID: 2024-25-327

Dyslexi is a mobile learning solution for young dyslexic children in Sri Lanka. The app empowers children aged 5–6 to improve their handwriting, pronunciation, auditory recognition, memory, and reading comprehension using personalized, interactive learning activities. Built with AI-powered feedback, gamified modules, and adaptive difficulty, Dyslexi supports early Sinhala literacy and makes inclusive education accessible for every child.

Core Modules & Features

Handwriting & Memory

Sinhala letter/shape writing, drag-and-drop activities, dictation, memory games, and real-time feedback.

Speech & Pronunciation

MFCC & AI for accurate Sinhala pronunciation, guided practice, and instant improvement feedback.

Adaptive Learning

Sentence scramble, picture storytelling, and difficulty that adapts to each child's performance and engagement.

Inclusive & Localized

Aligned to Sri Lankan curriculum, multi-sensory, easy progress tracking for teachers and parents.

How Dyslexi Works

Students engage in interactive Sinhala lessons, handwriting and speech exercises, and fun games tailored to their abilities. Real-time AI feedback motivates children and guides improvement. Parents and teachers can monitor progress and adapt support to each child's needs.
